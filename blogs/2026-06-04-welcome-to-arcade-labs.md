Arcade has launched Arcade Labs, a public platform for sharing research, prototypes, and experiments from the engineering team. The initiative launches with two projects: Omni, an MCP server integrating over 500 productivity tools, and Agent Library, a local-first memory system for AI agents. Nothing in Arcade Labs carries support guarantees or roadmap commitments, and some projects may graduate into the main Arcade product while others are archived.
